INT 21h AH=03H Auxiliary Input (DOS 1.0+)
─────────────────────────────────────────

Input:
    AH = 03h

Output:
    AL = Character read

Action:
    Read character (one byte) from standard auxiliary device.

    Normally standard aux is the first serial port. Under DOS 2.0 and higher,
    standard aux may be redirected.

Notes:

    DOS checks for CTRL+C and CTRL+Break keys and executes INT 23h if read.

    The standard auxiliary device usually does not buffer data. If data is
    coming in faster than the program can process it, characters may be lost.
